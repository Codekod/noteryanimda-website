Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

1. Introduction

The Noter Yanımda application ("Application") has been developed to enable users to easily access notaries in Turkey. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Authentication: Credentials provided by Apple Sign In or Google Sign In services when you use them

2.2 Location Information

  • GPS Location: Your location information is requested to find the nearest notaries
  • Location data is only used during searches and is not stored
  • You can disable location access from settings at any time

2.3 Usage Data

  • In-app search history
  • Favorite notaries list
  • Appointment records

3. Use of Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Finding and recommending the nearest notaries
  • Appointment management and reminders
  • Improving application services
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: Legal obligations or court orders
  • Service Providers: Technical services we use for application infrastructure (Supabase, Google Maps API)
  • Authentication: Apple and Google authentication services

5. Data Security

The following measures have been taken to secure your data:

  • Secure data transmission with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are stored hashed with bcrypt
  • Database access controls and firewalls
  • Regular security updates and audits

6. User Rights

Under KVKK (Personal Data Protection Law), you have the following rights:

  • Learning whether your personal data is being processed
  • Requesting access to your personal data
  • Requesting correction of your personal data
  • Requesting deletion of your personal data
  • Permanently deleting your account

7. Cookies and Tracking

The Application may use technical cookies to improve user experience. Tracking data is collected anonymously and is not used for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18.

9. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified through in-app notifications when significant changes occur.
