Yes, on-duty notary information is automatically pulled from the Turkish Notaries Union and is always current.
How can I make an appointment?
The appointment system is currently only active for member notaries. You can make an appointment by selecting a member notary.

Why does the app request location permission?
Location permission is used to find the nearest notaries to you. If you don't want to use this feature, you can manually select the province and district.
